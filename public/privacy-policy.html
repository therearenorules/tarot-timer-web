본 앱은 최소한의 정보만을 수집합니다:

🔮 Tarot Timer - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

1. Purpose of Personal Information Processing

Tarot Timer is an educational tarot card learning app that processes personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing app services and storing learning records
  • 24-hour tarot learning notification service
  • Storing personal learning journals and notes
  • Saving user settings and preferences

2. Personal Information We Collect

This app collects only minimal information:

  • Automatically collected: Device identifiers, app usage logs
  • User input: Personal learning journals, card notes
  • Settings: Notification preferences, language settings

3. Data Retention

Personal information is deleted without delay after the purpose is achieved:

  • Learning records: Until app deletion
  • Settings: Until app deletion
  • Personal journals: Until user deletion

4. Third-Party Sharing

This app does not share user personal information with third parties.
